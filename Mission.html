Our Mission

Our mission at Air Medics Rangers is to provide a safe and inclusive platform for youth to share their views and ideas on environmental conservation. We aim to develop engaging activities and workshops that not only fulfill volunteer and coop hour requirements but also instill a sense of responsibility towards our planet. Through our programs, young individuals will actively participate in fundraising initiatives and team-building events, both online and locally, to create awareness about environmental issues.
